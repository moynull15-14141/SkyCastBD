Cyclone Season Preparation for Coastal Bangladesh

May 16, 2026 · SkyCastBD Editorial Team

A preparedness checklist for households, schools, and small businesses in coastal districts.

Understand Warning Stages Early

Watch official warning levels and forecast tracks continuously. Early movement decisions reduce exposure to storm surge and transport disruption.

Build a Family Safety Kit

Keep drinking water, dry food, flashlights, power banks, first-aid supplies, and emergency contacts ready in one waterproof bag.

Protect Homes and Businesses

Secure loose outdoor items, back up important documents, and prepare continuity plans for shops, fisheries, and local service providers.
