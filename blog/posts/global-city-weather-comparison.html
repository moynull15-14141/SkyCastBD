Global City Weather Comparison: Dhaka vs London vs Dubai

May 14, 2026 · SkyCastBD Editorial Team

A simple international weather comparison for travelers, students, and business teams.

Dhaka: Humid and Rain-Sensitive

Dhaka often has high humidity and sudden rainfall bursts during monsoon months, affecting commute time and air comfort.

London: Mild but Frequently Cloudy

London weather is usually cooler with frequent cloud cover and light rainfall, requiring layered clothing and flexible outdoor schedules.

Dubai: Hot and Dry Extremes

Dubai shows high daytime heat and low rain probability for most of the year. Heat safety and hydration are critical for outdoor activity.
