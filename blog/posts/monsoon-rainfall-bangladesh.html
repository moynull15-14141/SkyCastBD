Understanding Monsoon Rainfall in Bangladesh

May 18, 2026 · SkyCastBD Editorial Team

A practical monsoon briefing for city commuters, families, and small businesses across Bangladesh.

Why Monsoon Forecasts Matter

In Bangladesh, monsoon rainfall influences transport, school schedules, logistics, and public health. Even short bursts of intense rainfall can cause road flooding in dense urban zones.

City-Level Rainfall Behavior

Dhaka commonly experiences heavy afternoon showers, Sylhet sees prolonged wet conditions, and Chattogram can receive strong coastal rain bands depending on wind direction.

Daily Planning Tips

Track hourly rain probability and wind speed before commuting. Keep emergency essentials for power cuts, avoid waterlogged routes, and monitor local alerts for severe weather.
