Privacy Policy

How SkyCastBD protects visitor information while delivering weather updates.

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Overview

SkyCastBD is a weather platform focused on Bangladesh and global city forecasts. We aim to collect only the information needed to operate the website, improve performance, and respond to user messages.

Information We May Collect

  • Basic contact details you voluntarily submit through the contact form, such as name, email address, and message content.
  • Approximate location data only when you choose to use browser-based location features for weather lookup.
  • Non-personal technical data such as browser type, pages visited, device type, and performance information.

How We Use Information

We use information to show weather forecasts, improve the user experience, respond to inquiries, maintain security, and troubleshoot technical issues.

Weather and Third-Party Services

SkyCastBD may request weather data from backend and third-party weather APIs. These services may process request metadata according to their own policies. We do not sell personal information.

Cookies and Local Storage

The website may use browser storage to remember preferences such as theme mode. You can clear this data from your browser settings at any time.

Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect submitted information. However, no internet-based service can guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

You may avoid submitting contact form data, deny browser location permission, or disable browser storage where supported by your browser.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact SkyCastBD through the contact page.
